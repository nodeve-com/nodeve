## severity - ramp - range

- good (widest desirable)
- better (middle)
- best (narrowest - bullseye)
- nominal (NULL - no severity)
- notice (low)
- caution (medium/elevated)
- warning (high/warning → nearLimit)
- critical (critical - Cutoff limit; unusable/unsafe, hard protection acts)
- fatal (failed/dead/explode)

## zone - named operating zone/band/mode - boolean

- off (switched off)
- idle (switched on, but not active)
- active (min = startup, max = shutdown)
- heating
- compressor

## Rating - curves and/or time limit_class

- continuous: (Normal DESIGN CAPACITY Nameplate, dynamic operational envelope curve), ACTIVE OPERATIONAL CAPACITY
- intermittent: minutes
- short-term: seconds (peak / Overload) ()
- survival
- protection_requirement

interval_kind: (measurable), (reference_point, rating, limits), (band, zone)
